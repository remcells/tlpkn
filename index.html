This is a two-player game using one device.
Players take turns in making moves.
attack:
deducts 5 points from opponent's health
power up +:
increases attack points (+2, +4, +6, +8, or +10)
each power up costs 5 stamina points
power up 0:
no increase in attack points
and loses turn to attack
stamina:
used to buy power up points
regenerates by 10 points after each attack
health:
indicates health status / survival rate
Last chicken standing wins 🏆
